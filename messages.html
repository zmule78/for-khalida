To My Dearest Khalida

Forever

Sayang,

There aren't enough words in any language - not in Indonesian, not in English, not even in Japanese - to express how much you mean to me. But let me try.

From the moment we started our thesis journey together, I knew there was something special about you. Your dedication, your kindness, your brilliant mind - everything about you captivated me. You weren't just my study partner; you became my best friend, my confidant, my inspiration.

I remember those long nights at East Indies, working on our research, sharing our dreams and fears. I remember how you celebrated my gold medal win with such genuine joy. I remember how you always had encouraging words when I felt overwhelmed. Every moment with you felt right.

When I finally told you how I felt at that cat cafe on December 1st, 2025, my heart was racing. And when you said yes, I felt like the luckiest person in the world. I still do, every single day.

You make me want to be better. You inspire me to work harder, dream bigger, love deeper. Your strength amazes me, your kindness humbles me, and your love completes me.

Thank you for being you. Thank you for choosing me. Thank you for every smile, every laugh, every moment we share. Thank you for supporting my dreams and letting me support yours. Thank you for making ordinary days extraordinary.

I promise to always be there for you, to support your dreams, to celebrate your victories, and to comfort you in difficult times. I promise to make you smile, to keep you safe, and to love you with everything I have.

This website is just a small token of my love for you. Every page, every word, every feature was created with you in mind. I wanted to create something that captures how special you are and how much you mean to me.

I love you, Khalida. More than yesterday, less than tomorrow. Forever and always.

With all my love,
Your Faiz 💛
